Advanced Topics in Robust Machine Learning, 2019 (Planning)

  • Course Description:
    This course focuses on advanced topics in robust machine learning, including deep learning with weakly-supervised information, adversarial machine learning, robust graph neural networks and robust generative adversarial networks. Students are expected to read advanced research papers and finish half-baked projects.

Introduction to Machine Learning, 2019 (Planning)

  • Course Description:
    This course focuses on basic topics in machine learning, which covers three major parts. (i) Theory: concentration inequality, rademacher complexity and minimax lower bounds. (ii) Model and optimization: support vector machines, convolutional neural networks, recurrent neural networks, graph neural networks, generative adversarial networks, variational autoencoder, and stochastic optimization. (iii) Application: natural language processing and healthcare data analysis. Students are expected to grasp an overview of machine learning and its applications.
