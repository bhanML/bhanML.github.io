Prospective HKBU PhD/MPhil students

    I am currently recruiting self-motivated students. Please send an email to bhanml@comp.hkbu.edu.hk if you have interest in getting involved with my research. Specifically, I am looking for students with the following qualifications:

  • highly self-motivated and always wanting to do better

  • have strong technical abilities in mathematics and developing algorithms

  • good at programming and running systematic experiments

    I prefer students having strong machine learning background *before* joining my group. If you are interested in my group, I strongly recommend you to read as many of the following books as possible before applying:

  • M. Mohri, A. Rostamizadeh, and A. Talwalkar. Foundations of Machine Learning. MIT press, 2018.

  • I. Goodfellow, Y. Bengio, and A. Courville. Deep Learning. MIT press, 2016.

  • S. Shalev-Shwartz and S. Ben-David. Understanding Machine Learning: From Theory to Algorithms. Cambridge university press, 2014.

  • J. Nocedal and S. Wright. Numerical Optimization. Springer Science & Business Media, 2006.

    During the PhD/MPhil period, I can provide you several benefits such as:

  • Must: Summer internship (each year) at qualified labs in industry (e.g., 4Paradigm Research/Alibaba Damo Academy/JD AI Research/IBM Research/Baidu Research).

  • Must: Research visiting (each year) at qualified labs in academic (e.g., RIKEN-AIP/UT-Austin/USYD/UMelbourne/UTS).

  • Must: Four-year graduation for PhD and two-year graduation for MPhil (good transfer station for US top universities).

  • Optional: Dual PhD degree between HKBU and UTS for highly-qualified candidates.

Prospective HKBU-BSc/non-HKBU researchers

    I also work closely with HKBU-BSc/non-HKBU researchers in various research projects. I am looking for HKBU-BSc/non-HKBU researchers who have (1) strong motivation, (2) good mathematical skills, and (3) good programming skills (e.g., experience in Pytorch or Tensorflow).
    There are three cases normally:

  • HKBU-BSc: Please take COMP7250 (Machine Learning), read above books, and discuss with me your research proposal.

  • non-HKBU PhD/MPhil/BSc: Please get the collaboration permission from your supervisor before contacting to me, and discuss with me your research proposal.

  • non-HKBU visiting researchers (from mainland China): Please address funding issue before contacting to me (e.g., CSC Scholarship/financial support from your own supervisor), and discuss with me your research proposal.

    After outputing some collaborations, I can provide you several benefits as follows:

  • HKBU-BSc: Recommendation letters (from qualified collaborators and me) to qualified universities (e.g., UT-Austin/UToronto/UTokyo/USYD/UMelbourne/UTS).

  • non-HKBU PhD/MPhil/BSc/visiting researchers: Full-time job recommendation to qualified research labs in industry (e.g., 4Paradigm Research/Alibaba Damo Academy/JD AI Research/IBM Research/Baidu Research).
